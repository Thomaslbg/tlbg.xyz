About me

Bonjour,

I am French and have been passionate about the digital world since I was young.

I worked 10 years in marketing mostly on acquisition (paid media, SEO, partnership, emailing) and analytics. I have worked for an agency (Effilab), a platform (Meta) and several startups (Jellynote, mobile.club, Rutabago).

In 2O21, during my time at Rutabago, our conversion was too low which hindered the acquisition I was managing. I pushed to work on the UX/UI of the site and became product manager and designer. I taught myself the methods and tools to use (Figma). Then in 2022, I felt I wanted to go even more into digital products and decided to learn to code. I joined Le Wagon for a 9 weeks bootcamp in July 2022 and have been coding every day since.
